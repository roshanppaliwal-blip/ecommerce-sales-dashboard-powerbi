# 📊 FlashCart Sales & Business Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end business analysis of an e-commerce company, **FlashCart**, using Power BI.
The objective is to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, operational efficiency, and marketing effectiveness to uncover actionable business insights.

The dashboard is designed as a **multi-page interactive BI solution**, simulating a real-world business environment.

---

## 🧾 Dataset Description

The dataset consists of multiple interconnected tables, representing a real-world e-commerce ecosystem:

* Customers
* Orders & Order Items
* Products & Categories
* Payments
* Delivery & Logistics
* Marketing Spend
* Returns & Refunds
* Customer Support Tickets

This structure enables **comprehensive, cross-functional analysis** across the business.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* **Excel / CSV** – Data handling
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning & transformation
* **Data Modeling** – Star schema relationships
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI calculations & measures
* **Power BI** – Dashboard development & visualization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### 🟣 Page 0: Navigation Hub

* Central landing page for seamless navigation
* Interactive buttons to access all dashboard sections

### 🔵 Page 1: Executive Overview

* Total Revenue, Profit, Orders, AOV
* Cancellation Rate & Late Delivery %
* Revenue trends over time
* Channel & city-level performance

---

### 🟠 Page 2: Product & Profitability Analysis

* Revenue & profit by product and category
* Profit margin comparison
* Identification of loss-making products
* Product-level return impact

---

### 🟢 Page 3: Customer Analytics

* Customer segmentation (VIP, Regular, One-time)
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* New vs Returning customers trend
* Orders per customer

---

### 🟡 Page 4: Marketing & Growth Analytics

* Marketing spend vs revenue trends
* ROI by channel (Google Ads, Influencers, etc.)
* Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
* City-wise marketing performance

---

### 🔴 Page 5: Operations, Delivery & Support

* Late delivery analysis by city
* Return reasons & refund trends
* Customer support ticket analysis
* Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)

---

### ⚫ Page 6: Business Recommendations

* Strategic insights derived from analysis
* Actionable recommendations for:

  * Revenue growth
  * Marketing optimization
  * Operational improvements
  * Customer retention

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* 📌 App channel contributes the majority of total revenue (~75%+)
* 📌 High-value products drive revenue despite lower volume
* 📌 Certain regions outperform others, indicating expansion opportunities
* 📌 Late deliveries (~15%+) impact customer satisfaction
* 📌 Returns are primarily driven by delivery and quality issues
* 📌 High ROI marketing channels outperform others significantly

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

* Increase focus on high-performing products and regions
* Optimize delivery operations to reduce delays
* Improve product quality to reduce return rates
* Allocate more budget to high ROI marketing channels
* Implement loyalty strategies for high-value customers
